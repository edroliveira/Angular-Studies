  • Interpolation allows you to make calculations as well, like the following: 3 * 6 = {{ 3 * 6 }}
  • You can also use functions inside the reference, like: 4 + 4 isn't equal to {{ 4 + 4 + getValue() }}
  • A cool feature of interpolation is that you can use boolean expressions, such as: {{ angularCourse && getLikeCourse() }}
